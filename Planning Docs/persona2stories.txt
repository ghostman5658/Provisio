Persona 2 User Stories

1. As a business woman, I need to have a rewards/loyalty system, so that I can get discounts on reservations the more I book/stay

2. As a business woman, I need to be able to look up my reservations, so that I can see which reservations I have already booked

3. As a business woman, I need all of my information to be saved within my profile, so that I do not have to re-enter every time I make a new reservation
